About DORA

Project Purpose

The departmental environment of YCCE handles vast amounts of critical information related to course registration, faculty profiles, and administrative procedures. DORA (Departmental Online Response Assistant) is designed to be a 24/7 central knowledge hub, bridging the communication gap within the department.

How It Works

DORA uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand user intent and provide relevant, context-aware responses. It draws from a custom-tailored departmental knowledge base to ensure accurate and immediate answers for students and staff.

The Team

This project was developed by a dedicated team of students as part of our Web Application Development project, focusing on scalable architecture, user-friendly UI design, and efficient backend logic.
