Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for courses?
You can register for courses through the university's main student portal. Registration dates are announced by the administration at the end of each semester. You can ask DORA for specific course prerequisites!
Where can I find my professor's office hours?
You can simply ask DORA "What are Professor [Name]'s office hours?" or check the Faculty Info quick link on the Home page.
What should I do if DORA doesn't understand my question?
Try rephrasing your question using simpler terms. If DORA still cannot help, you can use the Contact page to reach out directly to the department administration.
